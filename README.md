        <h2>Why Exercise is Important for You?</h2>
        <p>Exercise is crucial for maintaining good health, especially as we age. Regular physical activity can help:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>Improve your heart health and reduce your risk of heart disease</li>
            <li>Strengthen your bones and muscles</li>
            <li>Improve your balance and reduce your risk of falls</li>
            <li>Reduce your risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes, high blood pressure, and some types of cancer</li>
            <li>Boost your mood and cognitive function</li>
            <li>Help you maintain a healthy weight</li>
        </ul>
